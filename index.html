How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get bread/roll
  2. Get sliced deli meat(ham, turkey, roast beef, etc.)
  3. Get lettuce
  4. Get tomato
  5. Get mayo
  6. Slice bread/roll in two
  7. Place desired number of slices of deli meat on the slice of bread
  8. Place lettuce on top of the deli meat
  9. Place tomato on top of the lettuce
  10. Add desired amount of mayo
  11. Cover the top with the other slice of bread
